# JIE Mastery AI Tutor - Complete Platform Overview

## Executive Summary

**JIE Mastery AI Tutor** is an intelligent voice-powered tutoring platform that provides personalized, real-time educational support for K-12 and college students. Using advanced conversational AI, the platform delivers grade-appropriate instruction across multiple subjects, offering 24/7 availability at a fraction of the cost of traditional tutoring.

**Live Platform:** https://jiemastery.ai  
**Status:** Production-ready and operational
